About Michael

1 min · Michael Lewkowitz

::: entry-content

Hi!

My name is Michael Lewkowitz.

I am a founder, runner, and occasional coder.

I invest through Possibilian, serve as Steward at Krause House, and Chair at Windfall Ecology Centre.

Prior, I founded Clean Energy Developments (acquired by TSX:NRG), Co-Founded AmoebaCorp (acquired by John St. Advertising), and partnered and supported initiatives in social finance, venture capital, digital health, distributed systems, and community power.

I was born and raised in Toronto and now live in London, Ontario in the neighbourhood of Old South.

My life leans a little more possibilian, a little less dual.
