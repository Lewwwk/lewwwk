Venturing forward.

::: entry-content So now with intellectual models for aligning a venture around a purposeful response to a deep calling, and for designing a viable organizational system it begs the question – now what? Investment. Investment of capital… intellectual, social, and financial. In ideal clean slate this might look something like this: identify a calling gather a group of diverse though leaders and community members to explore/deepen the calling conduct an ’externalities analysis' reconvene a modified group to: uncover a responsive purpose concieve of a theory of change and anchor it with a Big Hairy Audacious Goal. conduct an ‘internalities analysis’ design the viable system reconvene a modified group to: craft strategic directions establish near-term action plans Of course this cannot be a rigidly linear/sequential process but rather is an iterative co-creative one. It is also a continual process of capital investment: intellectual, social, and financial… done in an integrated co-creative style. And that’s what I’m working on now. :::
