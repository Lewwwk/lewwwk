(Stafford Beer’s) Viable Systems Model, or [VSM]{#SPELLING_ERROR_1 .blsp-spelling-error} is a model of the organisational structure of any viable or autonomous system. A viable system is any system organised in such a way as to meet the demands of surviving in the changing environment. One of the prime features of systems that survive is that they are adaptable.
