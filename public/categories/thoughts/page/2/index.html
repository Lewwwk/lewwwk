Back in 2001, Ervin Laszlo{.zem_slink rel=“wikipedia”} wrote about this in Macroshift. He found a common pattern in civilizations over the course of our history. Either they breakthrough based on a new combo of technology and mindset or they breakdown. While breakdown is alarming, it’s been pretty common in the history of civilization.
