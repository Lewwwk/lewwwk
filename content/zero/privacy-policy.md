**Zero — Personal Agent**
Last updated: March 3, 2026

## Overview

Zero is a personal AI agent operated exclusively by its owner. It is not a public service and does not collect, process, or store data from third parties.

## Data Access

Zero accesses the following services on behalf of its owner through authenticated OAuth connections:

- **Gmail** — read-only access to email, compose drafts (not send)
- **Google Calendar** — read-only access to calendar events

No data from these services is shared with third parties. Data is processed in-memory for the purpose of generating summaries and drafts, and is not retained beyond the owner's active session state.

## Data Storage

- Session state and preferences are stored in a private database accessible only to the owner
- No personal data is sold, shared, or disclosed to any third party
- No analytics or tracking services are used

## Third-Party Services

Zero uses the Anthropic API (Claude) for language processing. Prompts sent to the API do not contain raw credentials. Anthropic's data handling is governed by their [privacy policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy).
