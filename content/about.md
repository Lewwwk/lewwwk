I'm a [founder](http://igniter.com), runner, and occasional [coder](http://github.com/Lewwwk).

I invest through [Possibilian](http://possibilian.xyz), serve as Steward at [Krause House](https://krausehouse.club), and Chair at [Windfall Ecology Centre](http://windfallcentre.ca/).

Previously, I founded Clean Energy Developments (acquired by TSX:NRG), co-founded AmoebaCorp (acquired by John St. Advertising), and partnered on initiatives spanning social finance, venture capital, digital health, distributed systems, and community power.

Born and raised in Toronto. Now living in London, Ontario in [Old South](https://www.oscolondon.ca).
